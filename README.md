Retail Business Performance & Profitability Analysis
1. Analyzed retail sales data to identify profit-draining product categories and seasonal sales patterns using Python (Pandas, Matplotlib).
2. Calculated profit margins across product sub-categories and visualized the lowest-performing segments for business insight.
3. Evaluated correlation between sales quantity and profitability to understand inventory turnover efficiency.
4. Created monthly and yearly trend charts to uncover seasonal purchase behavior and performance cycles.
5. Exported a fully cleaned dataset and visual insights to build an interactive Tableau dashboard for strategic reporting and decision-making.
